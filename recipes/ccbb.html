Chocolate Chip Banana Bread

Description

Savor the irresistible delight of this chocolate chip banana bread, a moist and heavenly treat crafted by the culinary expertise of iggytakahashi and tested to perfection by the Allrecipes Test Kitchen. With a preparation time of just 15 minutes and a total cooking time of 1 hour and 10 minutes, this delectable creation is quick, easy, and ideal for any occasion. Whether enjoyed for breakfast, as a snack, or as a dessert, this 9x5-inch loaf promises a symphony of flavors. Mashed ripe bananas, a touch of milk, and ground cinnamon form a harmonious blend, while the leavening magic of baking soda and baking powder ensures a perfectly risen bread. The incorporation of white sugar, eggs, and an abundance of chocolate chips adds a decadent touch, creating a moist and flavorful experience with every bite. A testament to the artistry of home baking, this chocolate chip banana bread is the epitome of comfort and satisfaction, inviting you to indulge in a slice of pure delight.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Leaveners
  • Salt
  • Bananas
  • Milk
  • Cinnamon
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients in one bowl; the bananas, milk, and cinnamon in another bowl; and beat the butter and sugar in a third bowl, adding the eggs one at a time.
  2. Stir the banana mixture into the butter mixture. Mix in the flour mixture. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  3. . Pour the batter into a prepared loaf pan and bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick comes out clean.
