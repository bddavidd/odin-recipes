White Cheese Chicken Lasagna

Description

Indulge in the rich flavors of this decadent Chicken Lasagna, a delightful twist on the classic Italian dish. Boil and drain lasagna noodles to perfection, then saut√© onions and garlic in butter, creating a fragrant base for the luscious white sauce enriched with frozen spinach, minced garlic, and all-purpose flour. The creamy canvas of store-bought chicken broth and whole milk becomes a symphony of flavors with dried basil, dried oregano, black pepper, and fresh parsley. Mozzarella, Parmesan, and ricotta cheeses add indulgence, while leftover chicken finds a perfect home. Assembling the lasagna is an art, layering tender noodles with the sumptuous sauce, chicken, and cheeses. Baked to golden perfection, this Chicken Lasagna is a culinary masterpiece that promises comfort and sophistication in every bite, leaving your taste buds singing with delight.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Butter
  • Vegetables
  • Garlic
  • Flour
  • Broth
  • Milk
  • Seasonings and herbs
  • Cheeses
  • Chicken

Steps

  1. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles.
  2. Cook the onion and garlic in butter, then whisk in the flour. Add the broth, milk, and salt.
  3. Add some of the mozzarella and Parmesan. Season with basil, oregano, and pepper. Set aside.
  4. Assemble the lasagna according to the recipe.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven.
